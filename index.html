Rock Paper Scissors

How to Play

Rock-Paper-Scissors is a simple hand game usually played between two people. The game has three possible outcomes other than a tie:

  • Rock vs Paper: Paper covers Rock, so Paper wins.
  • Rock vs Scissors: Rock crushes Scissors, so Rock wins.
  • Paper vs Scissors: Scissors cut Paper, so Scissors win.

When both players choose the same item, it's a tie.

After each round, you can choose to play again or start a new game to reset the scores.
